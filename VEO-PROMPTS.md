# Roam — Detailed Video Prompts for Veo

Each prompt below is self-contained. Copy-paste directly into Veo. Generate each scene separately, then stitch in sequence.

---

## SCENE 1: "The Flatness" (5 seconds)

**Prompt:**
Cinematic 4K footage, anamorphic lens, shallow depth of field. Interior of a small modern apartment at dusk. A young man in his late 20s wearing a gray hoodie sits hunched at a minimalist desk. The only light sources are a laptop screen casting cold blue light on his face and a phone face-down beside it. Behind him, a large window shows golden hour light outside — warm amber sunset filtering through half-closed blinds, casting long horizontal shadows across the wall. He does not look at the window. His expression is flat — not sad, not angry, just absent. Empty coffee mug beside the laptop. Camera starts wide showing the full scene, then slowly dollies in toward his face over 5 seconds. Muted color palette except for the golden window light. Film grain. Aspect ratio 16:9. Mood: melancholic, still, quiet isolation.

---

## SCENE 2: "The Glow" (5 seconds)

**Prompt:**
Cinematic 4K close-up shot. Same young man at the desk. His phone, lying face-up beside the laptop, lights up with a warm pulsing amber glow — like something alive, breathing. The glow is organic, not a harsh notification. It pulses gently: bright, dim, bright, dim — like a heartbeat. The amber light reflects on the desk surface and softly illuminates the underside of his chin. His eyes, previously unfocused, shift down to the phone. A micro-expression: curiosity. His fingers twitch toward it. The cold blue laptop light contrasts with the warm amber phone glow. Camera is a tight close-up on his face and the phone, rack focus between them. The window's golden light in the background is still there, now echoing the phone's amber. Aspect ratio 16:9. Mood: a spark of something in the emptiness.

---

## SCENE 3: "Shoes On" (5 seconds)

**Prompt:**
Cinematic 4K, quick editorial cuts. Shot 1 (1.5s): Close-up of hands tying sneaker laces on a dark hardwood floor. The laces are white, the shoes are worn-in. Shot 2 (1.5s): A hand reaches for a door handle — brushed metal, warm amber light leaking through the crack around the doorframe. Shot 3 (2s): The door swings open outward. Golden hour sunlight floods the frame, overexposing for a moment before the camera adjusts. The young man steps out onto a concrete sidewalk. He puts in a single wireless earbud (white, right ear). The camera is behind him, shooting over his shoulder as the street unfolds ahead — tree-lined, residential, warm light on every surface. His silhouette against the golden light. He takes the first step. Aspect ratio 16:9. Mood: threshold moment, transition from dark to light, from stuck to moving.

---

## SCENE 4: "The Walk" (7 seconds)

**Prompt:**
Cinematic 4K, steadicam following shot with gentle handheld sway. The young man walks through a vibrant urban neighborhood — Capitol Hill, Seattle aesthetic: colorful murals on brick walls, independent coffee shops with string lights, mature trees with dappled sunlight on the sidewalk, vintage apartment buildings with fire escapes. Golden hour light bathes everything in warm amber. Camera follows him from various angles: behind (over shoulder, seeing the street ahead), side profile (passing a mural), front (walking toward camera, background blurred). At 3 seconds, he looks up at a building he's never noticed before — head tilts, slight smile. At 5 seconds, he passes a small park with a fountain, pauses for a beat, then keeps walking. His posture has changed — shoulders back, head up, stride longer. A subtle visual effect: very faint amber particles or motes of light float in the air around him, barely visible, like the city is waking up to his presence. Not fantasy — just the feeling of noticing. Aspect ratio 16:9. Mood: discovery, warmth, the world opening up.

---

## SCENE 5: "The Group" (5 seconds)

**Prompt:**
Cinematic 4K, wide shot transitioning to medium. Three young professionals (mid-20s, diverse, casual clothes) walking together through the same neighborhood. They are animated and engaged — one points at something across the street, another laughs, the third holds up their phone to show the others something on the screen. The phone screen shows a dark map with glowing amber and violet tiles — a heat map pattern. They're not looking at their phones obsessively — they glance, point, then look at the real world. One of them peels off to check a side street, then jogs back to the group waving excitedly (found something). Camera captures the energy of group exploration: friendship, curiosity, the joy of discovering a city together. Same golden hour light. Same Capitol Hill streets. They pass the same fountain from Scene 4, but now it has more amber tiles glowing on the ground around it (visual metaphor — others have been here too). Aspect ratio 16:9. Mood: social, energetic, belonging.

## SCENE 6: "The Map Remembers" (5 seconds)

**Prompt:**
Cinematic 4K, intimate close-up transitioning to wide. Night. The young man from Scene 1 is home, lying in bed in the same apartment. The room is dark except for his phone screen. This time the phone shows a dark map with a single glowing amber line — a path tracing the walk he took today. The line is beautiful, organic, like a vein of gold on obsidian. He looks at it for a moment. A small, genuine smile — the first real one. He closes the phone. The screen goes dark but a faint amber afterglow remains on his face for a beat. Camera slowly pulls back to show the full apartment — the same room from Scene 1, but now the window shows city lights outside. Where Scene 1 felt isolating, this feels like rest. He's the same person in the same room but something small has shifted. Final frame: fade to pure black, but a single soft amber dot pulses once in the center — like a heartbeat, like the phone, like Echo breathing. Hold for 1 second. Cut to black. Aspect ratio 16:9. Mood: quiet satisfaction, warmth in the dark, tomorrow is already there.

## BONUS SCENE: "Dog Walk" (5 seconds, optional)

**Prompt:**
Cinematic 4K, morning light. The same young man walks down a residential sidewalk with a medium-sized golden retriever on a leash. Soft morning sun, dew on the grass. He holds his phone loosely in one hand — the screen shows the amber map with a dotted suggested route. The dog pulls gently toward a side street. He glances at his phone, smiles, and follows the dog. Camera follows from a low angle (dog's perspective looking up at the owner). The sidewalk tiles beneath their feet subtly glow amber as they walk over them — like the ground remembers their steps. Warm, gentle, everyday magic. Aspect ratio 16:9. Mood: routine made beautiful, quiet companionship.

## BONUS SCENE: "The Errand" (5 seconds, optional)

**Prompt:**
Cinematic 4K, single continuous shot. The young man is mid-walk on a commercial street. He passes a small grocery store. His phone buzzes gently in his pocket. He pulls it out — the screen shows a small amber notification card: a shopping bag icon and the word "Milk" with an arrow pointing at the store he's passing. He laughs softly, shakes his head (as if thinking "of course it reminded me"), and turns into the store. Camera stays outside, watching him through the glass storefront window as he grabs a carton of milk. Through the window, you can see the amber glow of his phone reflecting on the glass. Aspect ratio 16:9. Mood: practical magic, technology that fits into life rather than demanding attention.

## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR STITCHING

**Color grading:** Consistent across all scenes. Dark shadows, lifted blacks slightly (not crushed), warm amber midtones, desaturated cool tones. The amber (#F4A261) should be the dominant warm accent throughout. Night scenes lean toward deep navy + amber.

**Transitions:** Simple cuts between scenes. No wipes, no dissolves except for the final fade to black in Scene 6. Each cut should feel like turning a page.

**Sound design (if adding audio):**
- Scene 1-2: Near silence. Maybe a distant hum of an air conditioner. The faint tick of a clock.
- Scene 3: The satisfying click of a door latch. Shoes on pavement. City ambience fading in.
- Scene 4: Full city ambience — birds, distant traffic, leaves rustling. A faint warm tone underneath.
- Scene 5: Laughter, overlapping conversation, footsteps in sync. The warm tone grows.
- Scene 6: Silence returns, but warmer. A single soft piano note as the phone closes.

**Voiceover (record separately, lay over):**
Scene 1: "You told yourself you'd go outside today."
Scene 2: "It's 5:30. You didn't."
Scene 3: *(silence — let the visuals breathe)*
Scene 4: "Roam is the app that says 'hey, let's go' — and means it."
Scene 5: "A voice friend for when you're alone. A shared map for when you're not."
Scene 6: "Your city is waiting. Put your shoes on."
