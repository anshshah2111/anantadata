# Evidence photos

Drop interview screenshots, DMs, Reddit comments, or signup notifications here.
They automatically appear as polaroid overlays on the Insight slide of `/pitch`.

Recommended:
- `founders.jpg` — appears on Slide 1 (Cover) as polaroid bottom-right
- `interview-1.jpg`, `interview-2.jpg`, `reddit.png`, `signups.png` — appear as
  scattered polaroids behind Slide 3 quotes at ~15% opacity

Any `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png` or `.webp` works. Keep each under 1 MB if possible.
