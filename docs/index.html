Meet Echo
A friend in your ear,
not a coach on your back.

Echo is a warm, curious voice that walks with you. It asks about your day, notices when you turn down a new street, and never once says great job. You do not need a cheerleader. You need a companion.

🗣️

Voice-First

Talk naturally while you walk. Echo listens, responds, and knows when to let the silence sit. Short sentences. Real conversation.

🗺️

Your City Unfolds

Every street you walk reveals more of your personal map. The fog lifts where you go. Walk the same route twice and watch it glow warmer.

🌙

No Fitness. No Tracking.

Zero mentions of calories, steps, or workouts. Roam is about discovery and connection. Your walk is the point.

How It Works
Sixty seconds to your first walk.

Open the app

No signup. No login. Echo is already there, breathing softly on your screen.

Tap "Let's go"

Echo greets you and asks one small question. The map starts tracking. Fog begins to clear.

Walk and talk

Talk to Echo or just walk. Discover new streets. Watch your map come alive with color.

Come back tomorrow

Your map remembers. Your streak grows. Echo saved a thought for you.

Put your shoes on.
